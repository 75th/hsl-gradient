HSL Gradient Stops Demo

Colors only

      background-image: linear-gradient(to right, #{hsl-gradient-stops(lime, red)});

Specify direction (default is 'nearest')

      background-image: linear-gradient(to right, #{hsl-gradient-stops(lime, farthest red)});
    
      background-image: linear-gradient(to right, #{hsl-gradient-stops(red, counter-clockwise lime)});

Many colors (stop markers overlaid)

      background-image: linear-gradient(to right, #{hsl-gradient-stops(lime, red, lime farthest, cyan, orange, blue, orange farthest)});

Add extra turns through the color wheel

      background-image: linear-gradient(to right, #{hsl-gradient-stops(lime, 1 lime)});

Saturation and Lightness are interpolated once, regardless of extra turns

      background-image: linear-gradient(to right, #{hsl-gradient-stops(red, 1 #000033)});
